## Question 1 [MCQ]

Suppose $f$, $g$, and $h$ are three functions. Which of the following statement(s) is/are **true**?

1. If $f = O(g)$ and $g = O(h)$ then $f = O(h)$
2. If $f = O(h)$ and $g = O(h)$ then $f + g = O(h)$â€‹.

(a) Only statement 1 is true.

(b) Only statement 2 is true

(c) Both Statements 1 and 2 are true

(d) Both Statements 1 and 2 are false

### Answer

(c)



## Question 1 [MCQ]

$g3(n) = n^3 + 100n~log~n$

$g4(n) = 10~log~{n}$

$g5(n) = n~ log~ (2^n)$

Arrange the above functions in increasing order of asymptotic complexity.

(a) $g4(n), g1(n), g2(n), g3(n), g5(n)$

(b) $g4(n), g1(n), g2(n), g5(n), g3(n)$

(d) $g4(n), g1(n), g3(n), g2(n), g5(n)$
